# Welcome to FXFlow

## What Is FXFlow?

FXFlow is your **personal trading assistant**. Think of it like a smart helper that sits between you and the world of currency trading, making everything easier to understand and manage.

At its core, FXFlow connects to a trading service called OANDA (more on that later) and gives you a clean, modern dashboard to see your trades, get alerts, and even let artificial intelligence help you make decisions.

You don't need to be a finance expert to use it. That's kind of the whole point.

## Who Is It For?

FXFlow is for anyone who wants to:

- **Learn about forex trading** without feeling overwhelmed
- **Track their trades** in one beautiful dashboard
- **Get AI-powered help** deciding when to buy or sell currencies
- **Automate parts of trading** so they don't have to watch charts all day

Whether you're a complete beginner or someone with experience, FXFlow adapts to how you want to use it.

## What Can It Do?

Here's a quick look at FXFlow's main features:

| Feature                | What It Does                                                                |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Dashboard**          | See all your trades, account balance, and profit/loss at a glance           |
| **Charts**             | View live currency price charts and place trades directly                   |
| **SmartFlow**          | Create trade plans and let SmartFlow handle entry, protection, and exit     |
| **TradingView Alerts** | Receive trading signals from TradingView and act on them automatically      |
| **Trade Finder**       | Scans the market for trading opportunities so you don't have to             |
| **AI Analysis**        | Ask AI to analyze any trade and explain what's happening in plain language  |
| **AI Trader**          | Let AI find, evaluate, and even place trades for you (with your permission) |

## How Does It Work?

Here's the simple version:

1. **You connect FXFlow to OANDA** — OANDA is a forex broker (like a bank, but for trading currencies). FXFlow needs this connection to see your trades and place new ones.
2. **FXFlow syncs everything** — Every couple of minutes, FXFlow checks OANDA for any changes: new trades, closed trades, updated prices. It keeps your dashboard perfectly up to date.
3. **You trade your way** — Place trades manually, use TradingView signals, let Trade Finder suggest opportunities, or hand the wheel to the AI Trader. You choose how much control you want.

> [!NOTE]
> FXFlow does NOT hold your money. Your funds stay with OANDA at all times. FXFlow is just the tool you use to manage and view everything — like how a TV remote controls your TV but doesn't store the shows.

## Practice Mode vs Live Mode

This is really important, so pay attention:

- **Practice mode** uses **fake money**. It looks and works exactly like real trading, but nothing you do costs real money. It's a sandbox where you can learn, experiment, and make mistakes without any consequences.
- **Live mode** uses **real money**. Every trade you make affects your real bank balance.

> [!TIP]
> Always start with Practice mode. Seriously. Even experienced traders test new strategies in practice first. There's no shame in it — it's the smart thing to do.

## A Word About Risk

Trading currencies involves real financial risk. Here are some things to keep in mind:

> [!WARNING]
> **Trading is risky.** You can lose money — sometimes a lot of money, and sometimes very quickly. Never trade with money you can't afford to lose. FXFlow provides tools and AI assistance, but no tool can guarantee profits. Past results don't predict future results. If you're under 18, please use practice mode only and talk to a parent or guardian before considering live trading.
