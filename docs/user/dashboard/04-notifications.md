# Notifications

FXFlow sends you notifications whenever something important happens — a trade fills, a signal arrives, or the AI finishes an analysis. Think of notifications like text message alerts that keep you in the loop without having to constantly check every page.

## The Notification Bell

In the top-right corner of the header, you'll see a **bell icon**. This is your notification center.

- If nothing new has happened, the bell just sits there quietly.
- When you have **unread notifications**, a small red badge with a number appears on the bell. That number tells you how many new notifications you haven't looked at yet.

Click the bell to open a dropdown showing your recent notifications, with the newest ones at the top.

## Notification Types

FXFlow sends different types of notifications depending on what happened. Here's what each type means:

### OANDA Connection

These tell you about your broker connection:

- **Connected** — FXFlow successfully connected to your OANDA account
- **Disconnected** — The connection was lost (you'll want to check your settings)

### TradingView Alerts

When a trading signal arrives from TradingView:

- **Signal Executed** — A signal came in and FXFlow successfully placed the trade for you
- **Signal Failed** — A signal came in but something went wrong when trying to place the trade

### AI Analysis

When you ask the AI to analyze a trade:

- **Analysis Completed** — The AI finished its analysis and the results are ready to view

### Trade Finder

When the Trade Finder scanner acts on an opportunity:

- **Order Placed** — Trade Finder found a setup and placed a pending order for you
- **Order Filled** — A pending order from Trade Finder has been filled (the trade is now live)

### EdgeFinder

When the EdgeFinder system takes action:

- **Trade Placed** — The EdgeFinder decided to place a trade
- **Trade Managed** — The EdgeFinder adjusted an existing trade (like moving a stop loss)

### Conditions

When a condition you set up gets triggered:

- **Condition Triggered** — A price level or trade condition you were watching has been reached

> [!TIP]
> Don't worry about memorizing all these types. Each notification includes a clear description of what happened, so you can understand it at a glance.

## Clicking on Notifications

Each notification is clickable. When you click one, it **deep-links** you to the relevant page. That means it takes you directly to the thing the notification is about.

For example:

- Clicking a "Signal Executed" notification takes you to the TV Alerts page showing that signal
- Clicking an "Analysis Completed" notification opens the AI analysis results for that trade
- Clicking a "Trade Placed" notification takes you to that trade in your Positions page

> [!NOTE]
> "Deep-link" just means the notification doesn't take you to a generic page — it takes you to the exact thing you need to see. It's like clicking a link in a text message that opens the right conversation, not just the messaging app.

## Toast Popups

In addition to the bell, FXFlow shows **toast popups** for real-time events. These are small boxes that slide in from the corner of your screen when something happens right now.

Toasts appear for a few seconds and then fade away on their own. They're designed to catch your eye without interrupting what you're doing.

If you see a toast and want more details, you can find the full notification in the bell dropdown.

> [!WARNING]
> If you're seeing a lot of red or error toasts, something might need your attention. Check the notification bell for details and head to Settings if you need to troubleshoot a connection issue.

## Keeping Things Tidy

After you've read your notifications, the unread count on the bell resets. Old notifications stay in the list so you can scroll back and see what happened earlier, but the badge only shows what's new.
