# NNNN — Short imperative title

## Context

What situation or constraint forced a decision? What was tried or considered? Two or three paragraphs at most. A reader a year from now should be able to reconstruct _why this was on the table_ from this section alone.

## Decision

The decision itself, stated plainly. One or two short paragraphs. If the decision has multiple parts, use a numbered list.

## Consequences

### Positive

- What this unlocks, enables, or prevents from going wrong.

### Negative

- What costs this imposes. Be honest. Every decision has costs.

### Neutral

- Notable side effects that are neither wins nor losses.

## Alternatives considered

- **Option A** — description. Rejected because …
- **Option B** — description. Rejected because …

## Follow-ups

Concrete work the decision creates. Each item should eventually be reflected as a TODO somewhere auditable (sub-phase plan, requirement, another ADR) — never "we'll remember."

- [ ] Task 1 (owner: role, due: phase N)
- [ ] Task 2
